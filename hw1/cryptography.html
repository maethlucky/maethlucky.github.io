Cryptography is the practice of protecting information by transforming it into a secure format that can only be read or accessed by authorized individuals. It is used to ensure confidentiality, integrity, and security of data in digital systems.

Types of Cryptography:

  • Symmetric-Key
  • Asymmetric-Key
  • Hash Functions
  • Blockchain
  • Digital Signatures
  • Ciphers
  • etc.
